❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are the prices on NexDeal accurate?
We show indicative prices and redirect you to Amazon/Flipkart for real live prices. Always check the platform for the latest price before buying.
Does NexDeal charge anything extra?
Never! NexDeal is 100% free. We earn a small affiliate commission from Amazon/Flipkart when you make a purchase — at absolutely no extra cost to you.
How do I request a product?
Use the "Request Product" form on our homepage. We add requested products within 24 hours and post on our Telegram channel!
Is my data safe on NexDeal?
Yes! We store cart and wishlist data only on your device. We don't collect personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I install the NexDeal app?
Scroll to the bottom of our homepage and click the Install button for your platform (Android, iOS, Windows or macOS). It's free!
