Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
ℹ️ NexDeal is a price comparison and affiliate marketing website. We redirect users to Amazon and Flipkart. We do not sell products directly.

1. Information We Collect

NexDeal collects minimal information to provide our service:

  • Cart data — stored locally on your device only, never sent to our servers
  • Wishlist data — stored locally on your device only
  • Product requests — product name and optional name you provide
  • Theme preference — stored locally on your device

2. Affiliate Links

NexDeal uses affiliate links to Amazon India, Flipkart, Myntra, AJIO, Nykaa and other platforms. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

When you click an affiliate link, the destination platform (Amazon, Flipkart etc.) may set cookies on your browser as per their own privacy policies.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — used for our product database and admin authentication
  • Vercel — our website hosting provider, may collect standard server logs
  • Telegram — for product request notifications and our channel
  • Groq AI — powers our AI chat assistant

4. Cookies

NexDeal uses only essential local storage (not tracking cookies) to save your cart, wishlist, and preferences on your own device. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

5. Data Security

All data transmission is secured via HTTPS (SSL). Admin panel is protected by Firebase Authentication. We do not store payment information — all purchases happen on Amazon/Flipkart directly.

6. Children's Privacy

NexDeal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Since we store minimal data locally on your device, you can clear your browser storage at any time to remove all NexDeal data. For product request data sent via Telegram, contact us to request deletion.
