About NexDeal

India's smartest price comparison platform — built by a CSE student to help every Indian save money on electronics and fashion.

I built NexDeal because I was tired of checking Amazon and Flipkart separately every time I wanted to buy something. As a CSE student, I decided to build the solution myself — a platform that compares prices instantly and honestly, with no fake numbers!

🎯 Our Mission

💰Save Money — Help every Indian get the best price on every purchase, no matter the platform.
✅100% Honest — We never show fake prices. We redirect you directly to Amazon and Flipkart for real live prices.
🤖AI-Powered — Our AI assistant helps you choose the right product for your budget and needs.
📱Mobile First — Built for Indian users on mobile — fast, clean and easy to use.

⚙️ How NexDeal Works

Step 1
Browse products on NexDeal — we show electronics, fashion and more from top Indian brands.
Step 2
Click Amazon or Flipkart button — we redirect you directly to the real product page.
Step 3
You buy at the real price on Amazon/Flipkart. NexDeal earns a small affiliate commission — at zero extra cost to you!

💛 Affiliate Disclosure

NexDeal participates in affiliate programs including Amazon Associates, Flipkart Affiliate, and Cuelinks. When you click our links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission.

This comes at absolutely zero extra cost to you. Prices are set entirely by Amazon, Flipkart and other platforms — NexDeal has no control over pricing.

We only recommend products we genuinely believe are good value. Our goal is to save you money, not cost you more!
